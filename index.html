Today's Menu

Leche Flan

A silky, melt-in-your-mouth custard dessert made from rich egg yolks, milk, and caramelized sugar. Its smooth texture and elegant sweetness make it a timeless favorite that instantly elevates any celebration.

Biko

A traditional sticky rice delicacy slowly cooked in coconut milk and brown sugar. With its rich aroma and satisfying chew, biko brings a warm, nostalgic comfort that feels like home in every bite.

Kutsinta

A soft, chewy steamed rice cake known for its distinct amber color and subtle sweetness. Often topped with grated coconut, this delicacy offers a simple yet charming flavor that keeps people coming back for more.
