KUTSINTA

Local dishes, ingredients, and food stories from Agusan del Norte

Overview

GieLan's Kakanin and Dessert in San Agustin, Surigao del Sur, offers a variety of affordable kakanins and sweet treats. It’s especially famous for its kutsinta, which comes in different flavors and toppings, making it their best-selling delicacy. Each bite is soft, chewy, and full of flavor, perfect for anyone looking for a classic Filipino dessert at a great price.
