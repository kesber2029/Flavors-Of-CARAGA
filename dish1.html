LECHE FLAN

P-2, Barangay 1 San Francisco, Agusan del Sur

Overview

Kanto Kombo, located at P-2, Barangay 1 San Francisco, Agusan del Sur, is a must-visit for anyone with a sweet tooth. Their leche flan is rich, creamy, and soft. a classic Filipino favorite dessert that melts in your mouth. Best of all, it’s affordable, so you can enjoy this delicious treat anytime.
